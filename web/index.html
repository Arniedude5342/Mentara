About Mentara

Mentorship, opened up for everyone

Mentara was born out of a simple observation: the students who succeed aren't always the most talented. They're often the ones who had access to the right guidance at the right moment.

I watched peers navigate their careers without direction, limited by geography, networks, or finances, while brilliant professors and professionals had hard-won knowledge to share but no easy way to reach the students who needed it most.

So I built Mentara: a place where any student, anywhere, can be matched directly with a world-class mentor in their field. No gatekeepers. No expensive fees. Just real guidance, one conversation at a time.

AM

"I'm a 15-year-old Indian American student at Eastlake High School, heading into my sophomore year. I built Mentara because I wanted to help other high schoolers find the mentorship and guidance I felt we were all missing."
