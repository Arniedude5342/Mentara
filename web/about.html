About Mentara

Mentorship, opened up for everyone.

Connecting students with professors and professionals who have already been there, so the right guidance is never out of reach.

My story

Why I built Mentara

Mentara was born out of a simple observation: the students who succeed aren't always the most talented. They're often the ones who had access to the right guidance at the right moment.

I watched peers navigate their careers without direction, limited by geography, networks, or finances, while brilliant professors and industry professionals had hard-won knowledge to share but no easy way to reach the students who needed it most.

So I built Mentara. A platform where any student, anywhere, can be matched directly with a world-class mentor in their field. No gatekeepers. No expensive fees. Just real guidance, one conversation at a time.

My Mission

To open up access to mentorship for every curious student, completely free.

Connecting people who want to learn with the people who can light their path, no matter where they start.
