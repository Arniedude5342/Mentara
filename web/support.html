Frequently asked questions

What is Mentara?

Mentara is a mobile app that matches students with mentors who've already walked the path they're on. Instead of searching through profiles, you tell us your goals and our AI pairs you with the right person, then you message and schedule calls right in the app.

When does Mentara launch?

Soon, on the Apple App Store (iOS). Join the waitlist on our home page and we'll email you the moment it's live.

How does matching work?

You share where you're headed and what you'd like help with. Mentara's AI then matches you with a mentor whose background and expertise fit your goals, with no endless browsing required.

How do I become a mentor?

We're building our founding mentor community before launch. Apply through the Become a mentor section on our home page. It takes under a minute, and we'll reach out before we go live.

Is Mentara free to join?

Joining the waitlist and applying to mentor are completely free. Full details about how Mentara works will be available in the app at launch.

How is my data handled?

We take privacy seriously and never sell your personal information. Read the full details in our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my data or account?

Email hello@mentara.me with your request and we'll process it within 30 days, as described in our Privacy Policy.

Still need help?

Reach us directly at hello@mentara.me. We'd love to hear from you.
